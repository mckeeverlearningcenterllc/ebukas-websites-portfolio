About Us

Ebuka's Websites

We are a Lagos-based web designing company with a team of professionals with over 20 years of experience building websites — now using the latest modern tools and AI-powered technology to deliver faster, smarter results than ever before.

Our mission is simple: every Nigerian business deserves a quality website that actually brings in customers — not just something that looks good. We build sites that work hard for your business, at prices that make sense.

Our team have a background in other areas such as in education with a special passion for schools, training centres, and institutions — but we love helping businesses of all kinds get online and grow.
